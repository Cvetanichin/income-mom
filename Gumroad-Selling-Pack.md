## 3. PRODUCT DESCRIPTION (Gumroad page copy)

### Short version (for the preview/summary field)

A structured 4-stage workbook that takes you from "something feels off" to a real, checkable 90-day plan — using the same results-based design logic professionals use to plan EU-funded projects, applied to your own life.

### Full description (for the main Gumroad description field)

**Most clarity tools stop at insight. This one doesn't.**

You've probably already done some version of a life audit. Maybe a journal, a quiz, a "values exercise" from a podcast. You finished it understanding yourself a little better — and then nothing changed, because understanding and action are not the same skill, and almost nothing teaches the bridge between them.

**The Clarity-to-Action Operating System is the bridge.**

It's a 52-page workbook built on the same discipline used to design EU-funded development projects: a clear problem statement becomes a Theory of Change, becomes a logframe, becomes a monitored, time-bound plan with indicators you can actually check. This workbook applies that exact rigor to your own life — not as a metaphor, as a method.

**Four stages. One continuous arc.**

**Stage 1 — The Life Audit.** Score seven life domains honestly, identify your three lowest, and trace exactly when the drift started — not when you noticed it, but when it actually began.

**Stage 2 — The Energy Diagnosis.** Most burnout advice fails because it treats all burnout as one condition. This stage helps you identify which of four distinct burnout patterns you're actually in — overload, depletion, misalignment, or autonomy loss — because each one has a different fix.

**Stage 3 — Values Clarification.** Not a generic "discover your values" exercise. A forced-ranking process that tells you which of your stated values you're actually under-investing in right now, and where the resulting gap is creating the most friction in your life.

**Stage 4 — The 90-Day Action Plan.** This is the stage almost every other workbook skips. You'll synthesize everything from Stages 1–3 into one clear objective, three outcome areas, checkable indicators of success, and a month-by-month milestone map — plus a pre-written Day 45 checkpoint and Day 90 review, so the plan holds you accountable instead of quietly disappearing into a drawer.

**What's inside:**
- 52 pages of guided exercises, fillable directly in the document
- A complete worked example throughout (Maria, a composite case) so you always have a model of what an honest, specific answer looks like
- A branching guide that tells you exactly where to spend extra time based on what your audit reveals
- Summary boxes at the end of every stage that carry your key findings forward — nothing gets lost between sections
- A built-in 45-day checkpoint and 90-day review so the plan is genuinely accountable, not just aspirational

**Who this is for:**
Purpose-driven professionals in their 30s–50s who want structure, not platitudes. If you're tired of self-help content that makes you feel understood for twenty minutes and changes nothing, this is built for you specifically.

**Who this isn't for:**
If you want a quick mood-lift or motivational read, this isn't that. This is a working document. It takes 60–90 minutes of honest effort to complete Stages 1–3, and the value compounds over your full 90-day cycle — not in the first five minutes.

**Format:** Digital download (.docx), fillable on desktop or via Google Docs/Word mobile. Print-friendly if you prefer pen and paper.

## 4. FAQ SECTION (for Gumroad page)

**How is this different from a regular self-help workbook?**
Most workbooks end at reflection. This one is built specifically to convert reflection into a checkable, time-bound plan — the same way professional project design moves from a Theory of Change to a monitored logframe. Stage 4 is the entire reason this product exists.

**How long does it take to complete?**
Stages 1–3 typically take 60–90 minutes in one sitting. Stage 4 is best done after letting your diagnosis settle for a day or two. The full 90-day cycle is meant to be lived, not rushed.

**Do I need to complete it in order?**
Yes — each stage builds on the last. Stage 4 specifically requires what you carried forward from Stages 1–3.

**What if I don't have a clear "problem" — I just feel vaguely stuck?**
That's exactly what Stage 1 is built for. Most people start with a diffuse feeling and leave with a specific, scored baseline.

**Is this a print product or digital?**
Digital .docx download. Fill it in on your computer, or print it if you prefer working by hand.
