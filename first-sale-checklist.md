# First Sale Checklist
**The First Offer Kit — Cvetanichin**

---

Work through these 12 steps in order. Do not skip ahead. Each step must be complete before the next one matters.

---

### Before You List

- [ ] **1. Offer statement written**
  Your one sentence: "I help [who] do [what] so they can [result]."
  It is clear enough that someone who does not know you would understand it immediately.

- [ ] **2. Product draft complete**
  All four sections written and reviewed. You have done at least one editing pass.

- [ ] **3. Three buyer outcomes defined**
  Concrete, specific things your buyer will know, do, or have. Not feelings — outcomes.

- [ ] **4. All assets ready**
  Any worksheets, templates, or checklists that go with the product are complete and included.

- [ ] **5. Product title confirmed**
  One clear, specific title. You have tested it by reading it to one other person and checking they understand what it is.

---

### Setting Up Your Product Page

- [ ] **6. Platform account created** (Gumroad or Payhip)
  Account set up with your brand email. Payout details added.

- [ ] **7. Product uploaded**
  PDF (and Notion link if applicable) uploaded to your product listing.

- [ ] **8. Sales page copy pasted in**
  Your sales page text is in the product description. Proofread once. Do not over-edit.

- [ ] **9. Price set to €37**
  Confirmed and visible on the product page.

- [ ] **10. Product set to UNLISTED**
  It is live but not publicly discoverable. Only people with the direct link can access it.

---

### First Outreach

- [ ] **11. Five personal messages sent**
  Written individually to five specific people — not a group message, not a broadcast.
  Each message says: "I made something I think might be useful for you (or someone you know). Would you take a look?" and includes the direct link.

---

### Public Launch

- [ ] **12. First public post written and scheduled**
  One post on whichever platform you use most. Uses one of your three sales hooks. Includes the product link. Set to go live once you have at least one piece of feedback from your five contacts.

---

## After The First Sale

These are not on the checklist — they happen naturally:

- Buyer receives the file automatically
- You receive a payment notification
- Reply to any questions within 24 hours
- Note any feedback — even informal comments — for your first product update

---

**When all 12 boxes are checked: your product is live and your launch is open.**

The rest is momentum.
