# Platform Quick-Start Card
**The First Offer Kit — Cvetanichin**

---

You need one platform to host and sell your product. Here is a simple comparison of the two best options for a first digital product.

---

## Gumroad vs Payhip

| Decision | Gumroad | Payhip |
|---|---|---|
| **Free to start?** | Yes — 10% fee per sale until you reach $1M lifetime sales | Yes — free plan with 5% fee per sale |
| **Ease of setup** | Very simple — product page live in under 30 min | Very simple — similar setup speed |
| **Design of product page** | Minimal, clean | Slightly more customisable |
| **EU VAT handling** | Handles automatically | Handles automatically |
| **Affiliate features** | Yes, built in | Yes, built in |
| **Upsell / checkout bump** | Limited on free plan | Available on free plan |
| **Best for** | Fast launch, large creator community, wide discovery | Slightly more control, European sellers |

---

## The One Recommendation

**Start with Gumroad** if you want the fastest setup and the simplest possible experience for your first product.

**Start with Payhip** if you are based in Europe and want slightly more control over your product page appearance, or if upsells at checkout matter to you from day one.

Both are free until you make a sale. You can always migrate later.

---

## The 5 Setup Decisions (in order)

**1. Create your account**
Use your brand email. Do not use a personal email address you would not want buyers to see.

**2. Upload your product**
PDF goes here. If you have a Notion template, include the public Notion link as a second file or add it in the "thank you" message.

**3. Set your price**
€37 for launch. You can change this at any time.

**4. Write your product description**
Paste your sales page copy here. Use plain text — no complex formatting needed.

**5. Set to unlisted first**
Do not go live publicly until you have sent the link to your five warm contacts and received at least one piece of feedback.

---

## After Your First Sale

- Gumroad and Payhip both send the buyer the file automatically. You do not need to do anything manually.
- Both platforms hold your first payout for a few days as a fraud prevention measure — this is normal.
- Payout to your bank account is straightforward on both platforms. Set this up on Day 1 so it is ready when your first sale comes.

---

## What You Do Not Need (yet)

- A custom domain
- An email marketing tool
- A landing page builder
- A checkout page builder
- Any paid plan on either platform

These can all wait until after your first 10 sales.
